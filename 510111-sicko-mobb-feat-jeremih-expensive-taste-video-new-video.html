Sicko Mobb Feat. Jeremih &quot;Expensive Taste&quot; Video

Sicko Mobb's forward-thinking bop sounds have spread throughout Chicago and beyond. And the end of last year, the pulled an especially mainstream collaborator from their hometown, Jeremih -- hot off his simmering Late Nights album -- and "Expensive Taste" has since become one of the Mobb's biggest hits. The track finds Jeremih experimenting with the quicker, melodic bursts of serotonin that are the Sicko specialty, and conversely, the Mobb are inspired to incorporate hints of Jeremih's sultry style of R&B. 

In the new video, the three Chi-Town crooners stand in front of a mesmerizing backdrop -- a wild, kaleidoscopic sensory overload of different designer patterns. It's a lovely new aesthetic, indicative that the sounds of Chicago are much more colorful than they were a few years ago.

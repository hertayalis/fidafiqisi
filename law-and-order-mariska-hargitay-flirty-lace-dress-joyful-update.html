Law &amp; Order's Mariska Hargitay models flirty lace dress in joyful update

Law & Order: SVU actress Mariska Hargitay looked incredible in her latest update about the NBC show. In an Instagram photo, the Olivia Benson actress, 59, ditched her police uniform and grinned in a glamorous black dress. It featured a sheer mesh sleeveless bodice with lace overlay and she wore her brunette hair in a straight bob. 

Mariska shared the snap to mark the last day of filming season 24, telling her followers how "proud" she was of the TV series.

"Current #mood. Last Day of #svu24 feeling incredibly grateful, and so proud of the work that we have done as a team, and the stories we’ve told. Can’t believe we are here. #ittakesavillage #teamwork #tenacity #vision #focus #perseverance #daretodreamdream #makeithappen #WhatARide profoundly grateful to be on this journey #squad #finishline #marathon," she wrote in the caption.

Showing their support for Markiska, her fans flooded to the comments section to write: "Congratulations to you all. You all slayed it. This season is amazing," and: "You guys have done an AMAZING job! It keeps getting better and better. Let’s go!! Season 25 and BEYOND."

Mariska often stuns fans with her show-stopping outfits - just take a look at the video below for proof - but she admitted that she has become more adventurous with her personal style as she's gotten older. 

"Sometimes at seven in the morning, when I’m taking my kids to school, fashion is just putting something on where I won’t embarrass myself. I’ll go safe, and I’ll just throw on a pair of jeans and a groovy robe and shoes that I know are cool," she said in an interview with The Cut, referring to her three children August, born in 2006, Amaya, whom she adopted in 2011, and Andrew, who joined the family less than one year later.

"But as I get older, I take more risks, and I think the biggest thing is, How do I feel in this?

"Maybe it’s the actor in me, but I dress so many different ways, and that’s what’s fun. I feel strong in Stella McCartney and Alexander McQueen blazers and a pair of killer black pants."

Mariska has been married to fellow actor Peter Hermann since 2004. The couple met on the set of Law and Order: SVU back in 2001 when the actor guest-starred in the police procedural drama.

How do you make the TikTok smash burger taco?

TIKTOK'S has become the home of many food trends including the newest smash burger taco.

Here's everything we know about the viral food trend and how to make it.

What is the TikTok smash burger taco?

TikTok's newest viral food hack is called the smash burger taco which is basically a smashed burger on top of a classic tortilla.

Though an original creator hasn't been linked to the meal, one user chef Brad Prose, also known on TikTok as @chilesandsmoke, posted a video on how to make it.

The instructional video garnered over 109.4k likes and 3.7million views.

His post read: "Smashburger Tacos are hitting the streets. Grab some tortillas and let's Go!

"I've been working this process for a bit and realized that smashing the meat with the flour tortilla directly on the griddle is very effective, and saves a few steps of preparation."

Since then, users across multiple social media platforms have been recreating their own versions of the food.

How do you make the smash burger taco?

Surprisingly, the smash burger taco takes about less than five minutes to make.

To make this easy meal, you'll need ground beef, flour tortillas, sliced cheese, chopped lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, and onions.

Lastly, you'll need ketchup, mayo, and mustard to make the special sauce, and salt and pepper to season the burger.

To create the smash burger tacos, you'll need to:

  • First, season your ground beef using salt and pepper or any seasonings you'd like.
  • Then you'll spread the seasoned ground beef onto one side of the tortilla.
  • Next, you'll place the beef and tortilla face down on a hot griddle until the meat is fully cooked.
  • Then you'll flip the tortilla over to cook the other side, while doing so, add your cheese.
  • Lastly, you'll take it off the griddle, add your toppings and mix the ketchup, mayo, and mustard to create the special sauce which'll be the finishing touch to your smash burger taco.

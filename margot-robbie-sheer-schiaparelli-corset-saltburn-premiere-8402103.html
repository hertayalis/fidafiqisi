Margot Robbie Said Goodbye to Barbie Pink in a Sheer Schiaparelli Corset

Ever since Margot Robbie established herself as the queen of Barbiecore, delivering the best promotional wardrobe for Greta Gerwig’s Barbie film in the process, it’s been a minute since she's graced us with an alluring red carpet look. But yesterday, the actress reminded us exactly why she became a modern-day fashion icon long before stepping into the Mattel doll's shoes while simultaneously kissing bubblegum pink goodbye (for now, at least!).

On Tuesday, the actress stepped out hand-in-hand with her husband, Tom Ackerly, at the Los Angeles premiere of Saltburn. Walking the red carpet event, she donned a dark brown sheer corset with a sweetheart neckline and bone detailing from Schiaparelli's fall/winter 2023 couture collection. As for her bottom half, she slipped into a pair of Manolo Blahnik pumps hidden underneath larger-than-life, wide-leg velvet trousers left unbuttoned. Keeping the accessories to a minimum, she complemented her look with a gold choker necklace and a smattering of gold rings, both by Roseark.

As for her glam, the blonde bombshell accentuated her glowing complexion with feathered lashes, rosy cheeks, and a glossy pink lip, and she styled her blonde locks in soft curls with a middle part.

Margot's husband (who she produced Saltburn alongside) wore a double-breasted blazer in a light brown shade over a black t-shirt. He paired the sharp look with black trousers and coordinating chunky loafers. Joining them on the red carpet was their co-producer, Josey McNamara, who wore a similar camel-colored two-piece suit.

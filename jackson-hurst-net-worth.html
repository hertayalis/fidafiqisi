Jackson Hurst Net Worth

Jackson Hurst:

$5 million

Jackson Hurst is an American actor. He’s been acting professionally since 2006. His first movie role was in Soul’s Midnight while his TV debut was in Inspector Mom. He later had roles in the films Striking Range, Cleaner, Have Dreams, and Will Travel. He next played the role of Joe Eagleton on The Mist. From 2009-2014, he gained mainstream recognition for his role as Grayson Kent on the Lifetime series Drop Dead Diva. Following this, he starred as Eric in the film Vanished – Left Behind: Next Generation. He also appeared as Steve Cioffi Jr. on Unforgettable and Corbin Duggan on NCIS: Los Angeles. His latest role was as Kirk Lacey on the miniseries Sharp Objects.

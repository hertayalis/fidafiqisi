Who are the voice actors for Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach?

Recommended Videos

Five Nights at Freddy’s has been a series notorious for jump-scares, spooky environments, and tricky puzzles — and Security Breach is no different. Taking the FNAF concept to the next level, Security Breach features a lot of new content for you to explore. The environmental storytelling wouldn’t be complete without some excellent voice acting, however, so if you’re curious who the voice actors are for Five Nights at Freddy’s: Security Breach, we’ve got you covered.

  • Marta Svetek: Gregory, Roxanne Wolf, Vanny
  • Heather Masters: Vanessa/Glamrock Chica
  • Cameron Miller: Montgomery Gator
  • Andy Field: Dread Unit
  • Kellen Goff: Glamrock Freddy/Sun/Moon
  • Michella Moss: Additional VO

There appears to be a discrepancy between Marta Svetek voicing characters that IMDB lists as being voiced by Jessica Tang and Michella Moss. The credits do not list Jessica Tang anywhere, so it is unknown what her involvement is with the game.

Those are the confirmed voice actors for Five Night’s at Freddy’s: Security Breach. Whether you loved or hated Gregory, Glamrock Freddy, and the others, the voice talents of these actors were indisputable in adding to a fantastic game.

Chris Jericho Threatens Bloody Segment For Adam Cole On AEW Dynamite This Week

AEW Double or Nothing is going down this weekend in Las Vegas. They have lot of room to go before selling out the T-Mobile Arena, and they have one more opportunity to hype up fans for Dynamite this week.

Chris Jericho logged onto Twitter to throw out quite a threat for Adam Cole. He told the former NXT Champion that there will be blood during their contract signing. In fact, The Wizard plans on signing the contract in Cole’s plasma.

Face to face, you can’t beat me @AdamColePro and you know this. I’m gonna sign this contract in your blood…. @aew #AEWDynamite

We will have to see how this contract signing turns out. If Chris Jericho has his way, then it will be one bloody scene. Then again, AEW fans should be very used to seeing blood on television at this point.

This should be a good Double or Nothing event, and the card is finally coming together. Only time will tell if AEW will be able to sell out the Las Vegas venue. You may check out Jericho’s tweet and the updated AEW Double or Nothing card below.

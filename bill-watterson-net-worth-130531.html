Bill Watterson Net Worth

Bill Watterson was born on July 5, 1958 in Washington, D.C. He is best known as the author of the popular comic strip Calvin and Hobbes, which ran from 1985 to 1995. Throughout his career, Watterson won numerous awards, including the Reuben Award for Cartoonist of the Year in 1986 and 1988. He is widely regarded as one of the most influential cartoonists of all time.

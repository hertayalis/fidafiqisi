JoeysWorldTour - Age, Family, Bio

Chef and food critic whose YouTube channel, JoeysWorldTour, contains humorous, improvised reviews of various fast food dishes. The videos on his channel have earned more than 97 million combined views.

Before Fame

He graduated from Mission College in Santa Clara, California with an associate degree in culinary arts/chef training in the early '90s. He began writing the blog Sublime Flavor in 2008.

Trivia

His videos have been featured on Comedy Central's Tosh.0 and truTV's Upload with Shaquille O'Neal.

Family Life

His real name is Joseph Hernandez. He has a brother.

Associated With

He has done collaborative videos with fellow food YouTuber Daymon Patterson, also known as Daym Drops.

Carlos Correa Bio, Age, Parents, Siblings, Wife, Children, Salary, Height

Carlos Correa was born Carlos Javier Correa Oppenheimer Jr.

Carlos is a Puerto Rican Professional baseball shortstop who is a free agent.

Carlos has played in Major League Baseball for the Houston Astros, who selected him first overall in the 2012 MLB draft, and for the Minnesota Twins.

Carlos has won several awards in his career. Some of such awards include Premio Juventud for Most Electrifying Athlete – Sports.

Carlos currently plays for New York Mets as a shortstop.

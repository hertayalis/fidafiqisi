Shop Missoma Earrings Loved by Celebrities.

As a fashion editor, I spend a lot of time scouring the internet to uncover the best sale items, unique pieces, and the latest trends. I come across a lot of well-styled people, all who, in addition to their effortless clothing, have a common denominator: polished accessories. Two celebrities in particular, Jennifer Lopez and Hailey Bieber, continue to impress me.

These A-listers never skimp on accessories, whether it be oval-shaped sunglasses, large-and-in-charge handbags, or statement earrings and lately, I've been engrossed in their ear stacking game. I first spotted Jennifer Lopez wearing hoop earrings from British brand, Missoma. Decorated with a malachite pendant, the 18-karat, gold-plated pair shines brightly beneath the singer's slicked-back bun.

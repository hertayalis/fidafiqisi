I Will Never Do Plastic Surgery But I Am Not Against Women Who Do It Fantana Discloses (+Video)@

Budding singer Francine Nyanko Koffi popularly known as Fantana in a recent interview with Sammy Kay on the Go online show has revealed that she has no problem with women who enhance their body through plastic surgery.

Fantana stated that it’s nice and she loves it. She however said that she won’t consider doing it as she is pleased with her body shape.

She had a word of advice for all ladies, she stated; “you don’t have to pressure yourself if you don’t want to and also went ahead to state that “don’t get your body done for a man, do it for yourself”.

She further added that at her age, there is nothing that would influence her to go and augment her body in anyway.
